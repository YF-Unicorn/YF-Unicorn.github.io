  9. 水浒传

    I just read a book called Water Margin. It was interesting. I have not yet read a book like this one before because It was not all vernacular. That made it a little hard to understand the sentences. But after a few days of reading the book, I gradually got used to how the sentences worked. I liked the book.
    This book is about how 108 people gather and lives on a mountain, how they defeated people from the royal court who wanted to arrest them. Then they became soldiers of the royal court. But at last, they got plotted because of all the things they did that were against the royal court. These 108 people each got mistreated by rulers that forced them to leave where they are living.
    There were a lot of characters in the book, and my favorite character is Wu Song. He is my favorite character because he is very brave. He fought a tiger that no one could defeat with bare hands. That tiger has killed a lot of hunters, and everyone wanted to kill it. He could also make people's enemies throw up their hands.
    The awesome stories from Water Margin will always stay in my heart.
